A bakery app with nutrition preview features, which can help customer easily choose product base on their nutrition need.

Role:
Sole UXUI designer   (Personal project)

Challenge:

Massive nutrition information for the products in bakery and cumbersome calculation of nutrition intake.

Research

I interviewed 4 participants with list of questions to understand the user's need and expectations

Pain points:

Persona:

Process

Brainstorm

Low-fidelity prototypes

Usability study

I did two round usability study, one for the low-fidelity prototype,  the other for the mockup. Find the opportunity to improve the product

Solution

The app provides nutrition labels allowing users to pick up the products they want easily. Users can apply these labels to filter for further selection.

The information separation of content and nutrition makes it less busy and more organized. And it enables the users to get the information with less effort.

The shopping cart only provides a checkout function but also nutrition calculation for chosen products.

The pie chart and bar chart illustrates the summary nutrition information of chosen product intuitively.
